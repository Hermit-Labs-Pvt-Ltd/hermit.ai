About Us

Hermit Labs is an IoT startup that integrates smart sensor systems, machine learning and AI-based analytics to continuously monitor asset and infrastructure condition, and enable predictive maintenance. Our mission is to help industries grow through real-time data fusion and analytics. With our advanced technologies, companies can know their assets’ health in real time and turn their data into quality-increasing and cost-saving actions.
